# Main-Text Biological Figure Map

The manuscript places the single-cell and multi-omics analyses in main-text Figure 5 and Figure 6.

Figure 5 covers the discovery scRNA-seq landscape and epithelial meta-program definition:

- 5a: major lineage UMAP.
- 5b: RCB-group cell-type proportions.
- 5c: canonical marker dot plot.
- 5d: epithelial subcluster UMAP.
- 5e: geneNMF Jaccard similarity heatmap.
- 5f: MP1-MP6 score violin plots.
- 5g: MP1-MP6 UMAP feature maps.
- 5h: MP biological annotations, pathways, and markers.
- 5i: HALLMARK overlap heatmap.

Figure 6 covers response-associated MP validation and tumour microenvironment analyses:

- 6a: MP-RCB0 and MP-RCB3 score comparison.
- 6b-c: MP-RCB odds-ratio heatmaps.
- 6d-k: CellChat source-state networks and ligand-receptor chord diagrams.
- 6l-o: RNA-seq and proteomic GSEA/GSVA validation panels.
- 6p-s: in-house and external pCR/RCB validation score plots.

The same mapping is encoded in `main_figure_panel_map()` in `analysis_r/R/mumoas_sc_multiomics.R`.
